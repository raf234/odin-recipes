Braised Short Ribs

Homepage

Description

This dish is to die for. It's one of my favorites and the prep can be done overnight, so it's perfect for meal prepping.

Here's 3 reasons you should give it a try:

  • It's nearly impossible to mess up.
  • You learn how to braise.
  • It tastes AMAZING!

You'll be sure to impress whoever you're sharing with.

Ingredients

  • 5 English Cut Short Ribs
  • salt
  • 30g oil
  • 1/2 white onion, rough dice
  • 1 carrot, rough dice
  • 1 stalk of celery, rough dice
  • 30g tomato paste
  • 500ml red wine
  • 200ml beef/veal stock (or plain water or more wine)
  • 2 bay leaves
